# October 2026 Almanac Overview

October is THE PIVOT MONTH. Despite Octoberphobia (1929, 1987 crashes), October is a "Bear Killer" that turned the tide in 13 post-WWII bear markets. Midterm year Octobers are ranked #1 for Dow (+3.2%) and S&P (+3.0%) — the BEST midterm month. October ends the Worst Six Months (May-Oct) and NASDAQ's Worst Four Months (Jul-Oct). MACD BUY signal watch begins October 1, triggering the Best Six Months entry and in 2026 midterm, the start of the 2.5-year Sweet Spot hold. Massive sector seasonality launches: nearly every sector goes long from October. Oct 2022 was best Dow month by points (+4000, +14.0%). Late October is explicitly called a buying zone for depressed techs and small caps.

## October Bear Killer [macro_context]

October has turned the tide in 13 post-WWII bear markets: 1946, 1957, 1960, 1962, 1966, 1974, 1987, 1990, 1998, 2001, 2002, 2011, 2022. Despite Octoberphobia from 1929/1987 crashes, October is a great time to buy. Oct 2022 was best Dow month by points (+4000, +14.0%).

## MACD BUY Signal Watch Active [timing]

Start looking for MACD BUY signals on October 1 for Dow, S&P, and NASDAQ. This triggers the Best Six Months entry (Nov-Apr) and in midterm years, the 2.5-year hold through the Sweet Spot. The MACD signal timing nearly triples Best Six results.

Midterm year Octobers since 1950: #1 Dow (+3.2%), #1 S&P (+3.0%), #2 NASDAQ (+3.2%), Russell 1K (+3.9%), Russell 2K (+3.2%). THE BEST midterm month. The pivot from weak zone to Sweet Spot happens here.

## Late October Buy Point [timing]

Late October is time to buy depressed stocks, especially techs and small caps. The Almanac explicitly calls this a buying zone. Oct 22-23 show S&P 66.7-71.4 with bull icons — the late-month recovery.

## Sector Seasonality Launches [timing]

Massive concentration of bullish sector seasonalities begin in October: Banking, Broker/Dealer, Computer Tech, Consumer Discretionary/Staples, Healthcare, Industrials, Materials, Pharmaceutical, Real Estate, Semiconductor, Telecom, Transports — nearly every sector goes long from October.

## Worst Six Months Ends [timing]

October ends the Dow/S&P Worst Six Months (May-Oct) and NASDAQ Worst Four Months (Jul-Oct). The transition from worst to best seasonal period is the single most important inflection point in the calendar year.
