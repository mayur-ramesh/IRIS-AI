# IRIS Translation Rules

How to translate Almanac D/S/N scores into IRIS-compatible signals:

1. Each trading day has three probability scores (D, S, N) ranging roughly 20-80.
2. Score >= 60: Historically UP day → maps to Almanac 'D' (Up) rating.
3. Score 41-59: Neutral/sideways → maps to Almanac 'S' (Sideways) rating.
4. Score <= 40: Historically DOWN day → maps to Almanac 'N' (Down) rating.
5. Bull/bear icons indicate particularly strong or weak historical patterns.
6. The S&P 500 (S) column is most directly comparable to SPY predictions.
7. Week 9 (Mar 23-27) had Almanac S&P scores: 38.1, 47.6, 57.1, 47.6, 47.6
   → Translated: N, S, S, S, S (mostly neutral-to-bearish).
8. Almanac achieved ~73% directional accuracy in Week 9 vs IRIS at 50%.
